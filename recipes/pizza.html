Pizza Recipes

“Description”

For the Dough:

  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 envelope instant dry yeast (about 2 1/4 teaspoons)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water, 110°F
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus 2 teaspoons

For the Sauce:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste/li>
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Toppings:

  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  1. Prepare the Dough
    1. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine 3 1/2 cups of flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
    2. Add Wet Ingredients: Add the water and 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Mix until the dough comes together.
    3. Knead the Dough: Turn the dough out onto a floured surface. Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic. Add more flour if the dough is too sticky.
    4. First Rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, turning to coat. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the Sauce
    1. Combine Sauce Ingredients: In a medium bowl, mix together the tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, and pepper.
    2. Adjust Seasoning: Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning if necessary. Set aside.
  3. Preheat the Oven
    1. Heat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  4. Shape the Dough
    1. Prepare the Surface: Lightly flour your work surface. Punch down the dough and divide it into 2 equal portions if making two pizzas.
    2. Shape the Dough: Roll out or stretch the dough into your desired shape and thickness. Transfer the shaped dough onto a piece of parchment paper if using a pizza stone or onto a lightly oiled baking sheet.
  5. Assemble the Pizza
    1. Add Sauce: Spread a thin layer of the prepared sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    2. Add Cheese: Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
    3. Add Toppings: Add your favorite toppings evenly over the cheese.
  6. Bake the Pizza
    1. Transfer to Oven: If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizza (with the parchment paper) onto the preheated stone. If using a baking sheet, place it directly in the oven.
    2. Bake: Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
    3. Cool: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
